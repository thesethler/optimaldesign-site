Virginia Senate Bill 531

The 2027 Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) Shift

On July 1, 2027, Virginia transforms residential real estate by legalizing detached accessory structures (like backyard homes, cottage flats, and garage apartments) by-right statewide. This wipes away historical occupancy rules and long municipal public hearings.

This is a massive new opportunity to turn empty property lines you already own into predictable rental income, family living spaces, or significantly higher resale valuations.

However, city codes, lot coverage thresholds, and yard limits will still dictate structural margins. Before you hire a contractor, our clean property feasibility reports ensure your yard footprint is perfectly calculated, formatted, and ready to pass local ordinances.
