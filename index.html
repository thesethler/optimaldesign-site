Virginia Senate Bill 531

The 2027 Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) Shift

On July 1, 2027, Virginia transforms residential real estate by legalizing detached accessory structures by-right statewide. This wipes away historical occupancy restrictions and municipal public hearings.

However, local geographic limits still dictate structural margins. Our specialized spatial data reports ensure your lot footprint is calculated, formatted, and positioned to submit on Day One—bypassing the inevitable municipal permit backlogs.
